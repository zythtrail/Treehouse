Climb the Treehouse: Start Collecting Nuts Today!

Treehouse

Welcome to the Treehouse Protocol Rewards Guide!

Are you ready to transform your stash into a treasure trove nestled high above the towering Treehouse?

Grab your digital climbing gear and your ETH—it’s time to scale new heights and harvest some juicy rewards. 

The World of Treehouse Nuts

Forget about boring points! Here at Treehouse, you earn Nuts! Trust us; these nuts aren’t something you stash away and forget. They’re part of something much bigger. 

Here’s how it works:

  • Hold Your Ground: Holding tETH? Great! For every 0.1 ETH worth of tETH held each day, you’ll squirrel away with 1 Treehouse Nut.
    A simple 0.2 ETH gets you 2 Nuts daily—no crumbs get left behind!
  • Plant your LP: Help support tETH’s liquidity! For every 0.1 ETH worth of tETH/wstETH LP token staked, you’ll squirrel away with 1 Treehouse Nut too!
    No, that wasn’t a typo. More stakes? More Nuts!

Note that the minimum staked amount to qualify for the GoNuts rewards campaign is 0.02 ETH.

Go Nuts with Boosts

Here at Treehouse, we’ve hidden loads of boosts all over to help you collect Nuts:

Patience is a virtue, especially when it’s measured in nuts! We reward the patience of our squirrels.

Stand to unlock additional rewards the longer you hold onto your tETH or keep your tETH/wstETH LP tokens staked.

How to hold tETH? Either mint it through our app, or do a swap from the LP pool with your existing wstETH!

The Treehouse Referral System

Every time you invite someone to our Treehouse, you both get a chance to hoard extra Nuts! 

Here’s the crunchy deal: Both you and the new squirrel will pocket a 5% bonus on all the base Treehouse Points the new squirrel gathers. Still don’t get it?

Picture this: You’re already busy storing Nuts by holding or staking in Treehouse. Now, by simply calling over your squirrel friends, you can significantly boost your stash. 

For each friend that scampers into the scene and starts collecting Nuts, they snag a 5% bonus on their earnings, and you get the same boost from their haul!

Referral bonuses do not enjoy Treehouse Points Boost

Be a part of The Squirrel Council

Introducing The Squirrel Council NFT—your ticket to doubling up on rewards and gaining exclusive insights into what we are building! 

Be a part of the Treehouse Squirrel Council and reap more nuts!

  • 100% Boost: Owning the Treehouse Squirrel Council NFT automatically increases your daily Nut collection, boosting your earning potential in the Treehouse.
  • Phygital Book: Receive One Rate To Rule Them All, a book about how we are revolutionizing the digital assets market by introducing fixed income primitives! 

Meet Our Partners: Boost Your Nuts through our NFT & LP Collaborations!

Building Treehouse requires loads of help, that’s why we’re teaming up with some of the biggest names in the space. Check out how you can supercharge your rewards through our partners!

NFT Partners: Our NFT partnerships allow you to gain additional boosts by holding specific NFTs alongside your tETH or staked tETH/wstETH LP tokens. 

LP Partners: Anchor the roots of our ecosystem by providing liquidity with our LP partners, and earn even more Treehouse Nuts:

Holding multiple NFTs of the same affiliated NFT collection will have a stacked boost effect equivalent to the boost of a single NFT times the number of NFTs held in that wallet, capped at a maximum of 3 per collection. 

Holding NFTs from different affiliated collections will result in a stacked boost effect, equal to the combined sum of boost values from each NFT collection.

The Treehouse NFT will not enjoy the stacked boost effect if the user holds multiple Treehouse NFTs.

LP Partners’ boost of 100% only applies to the base nuts earned through the LP Staking.

Well, that’s the scoop on collecting nuts here at Treehouse! Keep stacking, and prepare for an adventure like no other!

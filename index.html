THE TREEHOUSE PROTOCOL

tAsset powers Decentralized Offered Rates (DOR)

The Treehouse Protocol is a consensus mechanism that powers Decentralized Offered Rates (DOR), a new fixed-income primitive for benchmark rate setting. DOR enables dApps to rely on accurate reference rates, an important pillar of financial markets.
